Our Story

Built Around the Table

Savory began in a small Seattle kitchen in 2019. Our founder, a former restaurant chef turned home cook, grew frustrated that most recipe websites felt like grocery stores — cluttered, ad-heavy, transactional. She wanted something that felt like a well-designed food magazine.

So she built it. What started as a personal project became a destination for cooks who care — people who want to eat well without fuss, who believe lunch deserves as much thought as dinner, and who know the difference a sharp knife and good olive oil makes.

Today, Savory is a team of eight food lovers, recipe developers, and photographers based in Seattle, Washington. Every recipe is tested multiple times in home kitchens, not professional ones, because that's where our readers cook.

What We Stand For

Our Values

🔬

Rigorously Tested

Every recipe goes through a minimum of three rounds of testing in real home kitchens before it's published. If it doesn't work on a Tuesday night after work, it doesn't make the cut.

🌿

Seasonally Inspired

We think about food through the lens of seasons. Recipes evolve with what's fresh, what's abundant, and what tastes best right now — not what's on a content calendar.

📖

Editorial First

Savory is a publication, not a platform. We care about words, photography, and design as much as ingredients. Beautiful food deserves beautiful presentation.

🤝

Community-Centered

Our readers are our co-authors. Tips, adaptations, and stories from the Savory community shape new recipes every month. The table is always set for more guests.

⚖️

Honest Ingredients

We use real food: butter, salt, cream, olive oil, wine. We don't pretend substitutions are equally delicious when they're not. We trust you to make your own choices.

✨

Beauty in the Everyday

Tuesday's pasta deserves as much care as Saturday's roast. We're here to remind you that a great lunch — made with intention — is a small act of joy.
