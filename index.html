Kitchen Wisdom

Cooking Tips

Smart techniques to elevate every meal you make.

📋

Meal Prep Mastery

Batch-cook grains, proteins, and roasted vegetables on Sundays. Store in separate containers and mix-and-match throughout the week to create four distinct meals with minimal effort.

🥗

Healthy Ingredient Swaps

Replace heavy cream with coconut milk in soups. Use Greek yogurt instead of sour cream in dressings. Swap refined pasta for legume-based noodles — same comfort, more nutrition.

🔪

Kitchen Essentials

A sharp chef's knife, a cast iron skillet, and quality kosher salt are the holy trinity of a great kitchen. Invest in these three things before anything else.

⚡

Quick Cooking Hacks

Salt your pasta water generously — it should taste like the sea. Always rest your proteins after cooking. Brown your onions low and slow; they take 20 minutes, not five.
