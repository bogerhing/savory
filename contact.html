Common Questions

FAQ

Can I submit my own recipe?

We love hearing from our community! While we don't publish reader recipes directly, your ideas often inspire our editorial calendar. Send us your suggestions via the contact form.

How do I save a recipe?

Recipe saving is coming soon! In the meantime, you can bookmark any page in your browser, or sign up for our newsletter to get our curated weekly picks delivered to you.

Are you open to partnerships?

Yes — we work with select kitchen brands, food producers, and culinary organizations whose values align with ours. Please use the contact form with "Collaboration Inquiry" as the subject.

Can I use your recipes commercially?

Our recipes are for personal use. Commercial use, republication, or adaptation requires written permission. Please review our Terms of Use or contact us directly.
